FAQ

Questions People Usually Ask Before Starting

A quick answer to the most common concerns around timelines, content, support, and whether the work is a fit.

Do you only work with tourism businesses?

Short answer: tourism is the specialty, but not the limit.

Tourism, hospitality, and local businesses are the strongest fit, especially where trust, local visibility, and direct inquiries matter.

How long does a website usually take?

Most small projects move fast once content is ready.

Timeline depends on scope, but a focused brochure-style website can usually move much faster than a larger SEO or booking-led build.

Can you help if I do not have content ready yet?

Yes. You do not need everything figured out first.

If photos, text, or offer details are still messy, I can help shape the structure and guide what is needed so the project does not stall.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes. Support is part of the delivery, not an afterthought.

Each package includes a support window, and ongoing improvements can be planned separately if you want continued SEO, updates, or automation help.
